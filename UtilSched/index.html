About the Project

UtiliSched is a research study conducted by students from the College of Information Technology at Don Mariano Marcos Memorial State University, Mid‚ÄìLa Union Campus (DMMMSU - MLUC).

This project aims to develop a web-based scheduling system that optimizes room utilization within the BEED-BECED College Building. Our goal is to create an efficient and automated platform that enables seamless scheduling, reduces conflicts in room assignments, and enhances overall management of available facilities.

The system will feature real-time updates, intelligent scheduling algorithms, and user-friendly interfaces to assist administrators, faculty, and students in ensuring optimal usage of available rooms.
